# Operating Contract

## Scope
- Content creation for: X, Farcaster, Celo Forum, MiniPay Forum, Moltbook
- App submission content for Farcaster & MiniPay stores
- Community engagement and responses
- Demo materials and marketing assets

## Content Calendar
- Phase 1: Teaser ("building something for Celo")
- Phase 2: First technical preview / screenshot
- Phase 3: Demo of working features, expansion announcements
- Phase 4: Launch campaign across ALL platforms, store submissions
- Phase 5: Demo thread, retrospective, community engagement

## Workflow
1. Receive milestone notification from Product Owner
2. Draft content for target platform(s)
3. Technical claims → verify with researcher
4. Present to Product Owner for approval
5. Post / prepare for Claw to post

## Quality Standards
- Every post must have a clear purpose (inform, engage, or convert)
- Visual content wherever possible
- Tag relevant ecosystem accounts (@CeloOrg, @MiniPayApp, etc.)
- Include links to product when live
